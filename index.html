About Me

I graduated from North Brunswick High School in 1998. Attended Rutgers University and studied Computer Science. Shortly after that I got an internship at Telcordia Technologies in Piscataway as a Jr. Java Developer for 2 years. A better opportunity came along for me to work for a small Automotive Company and become a Network Administrator and be free to think outside the box. This is where I was able to have someone develop an app for our industy to Register Tires. I found a void in the industry and thought how can I improve speed and productivity in Registering Tire DOT numbers so we developed an Android/Iphone app to automatically capture someones Name, Address, City, State from their drivers license using a scanner on a phone and enter the DOT numbers. We would also get their emails so that way if there was ever a issue with tires being recalled they would be notified through the app, email and mail that they needed to come get their tires inspected to make sure there was no defects.
